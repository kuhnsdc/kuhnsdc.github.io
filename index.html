Weekly Results Shooter Standings
  1. Each member who participates puts in $20 at the beginning. At the end of the season, the winning team gets their money back ($120 total if all 6 are in). The remaining $240 is spent on a bbq for all three teams (food, drinks, everything). Losing team has to do the shopping.
  2. Winner is determined by grand total of all rounds shot. All 6 scores count so most possible points are 300 per week per team.
  3. If a shooter misses a Station 7 target there is a one dollar penalty per round. This money will go to the best overall shooter and will be tracked by the team captains.
  4. Missed nights: if a banked score is used that score counts, if a sub is used that score counts
  5. Trash talk and insults are highly encouraged
